<h1 align="center">Colosseum</h1>

<h2 align="center">Controls</h2>

- Up: W
- Left: A
- Down: S
- Right: D
- Attack: Left Shift
- Start/Restart: Space

<h2 align="center">Colosseum Description</h2>

This is a simple arena-game where you play as a knight and have to fight off increasing amounts of skeletons! You have a wide circle attack and as the waves go on, the skeletons increase. I'm using simple collision detection and enemy lists to add these skeletons and delete them. The enemies will always close the distance between them and the player using simple math operations.
